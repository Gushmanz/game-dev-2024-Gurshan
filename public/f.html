Weekly Food Plan

Monday

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs (3 eggs, 18g protein) + Greek yogurt (200g, 20g protein)
Lunch: Grilled chicken breast (150g, 35g protein) + quinoa (100g cooked, 4g protein)
Dinner: Baked salmon (200g, 40g protein) + steamed broccoli (100g, 3g protein)
Snacks: Protein shake (1 scoop, 25g protein) + almonds (30g, 6g protein)

Tuesday

Breakfast: Oatmeal with protein powder (30g oats, 1 scoop protein, 20g protein)
Lunch: Turkey breast sandwich (150g turkey, 30g protein) + whole grain bread (2 slices, 8g protein)
Dinner: Beef stir-fry (150g beef, 35g protein) + mixed veggies (100g, 3g protein)
Snacks: Cottage cheese (200g, 25g protein) + carrot sticks

Wednesday

Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, and protein powder (1 scoop, 25g protein)
Lunch: Lentil salad (200g lentils, 18g protein) + feta cheese (50g, 12g protein)
Dinner: Grilled shrimp (200g, 40g protein) + brown rice (100g cooked, 2.5g protein)
Snacks: Hard-boiled eggs (2 eggs, 12g protein) + a handful of nuts

Thursday

Breakfast: Protein pancakes (1 serving, 30g protein)
Lunch: Quinoa bowl with black beans (150g, 10g protein) + chicken (100g, 25g protein)
Dinner: Pork tenderloin (200g, 40g protein) + sweet potato (100g, 2g protein)
Snacks: Greek yogurt (200g, 20g protein) + berries

Friday

Breakfast: Chia pudding with almond milk and protein powder (1 scoop, 25g protein)
Lunch: Tuna salad (150g tuna, 35g protein) + whole grain crackers (30g, 3g protein)
Dinner: Grilled chicken thighs (200g, 45g protein) + mixed greens salad
Snacks: Protein bar (20g protein)

Saturday

Breakfast: Omelette with cheese and veggies (3 eggs, 18g protein)
Lunch: Falafel wrap (3 falafels, 15g protein) + hummus (50g, 5g protein)
Dinner: Baked cod (200g, 40g protein) + asparagus (100g, 3g protein)
Snacks: Edamame (100g, 11g protein) + a scoop of peanut butter (8g protein)

Sunday

Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait with granola and berries (200g yogurt, 20g protein)
Lunch: Chicken Caesar salad (150g chicken, 35g protein)
Dinner: Turkey meatballs (200g, 40g protein) + zucchini noodles
Snacks: Cottage cheese (200g, 25g protein) + pineapple
